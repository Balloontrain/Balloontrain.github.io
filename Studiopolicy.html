Studio Policy

Tuition & Billing

  • Payment is due at the first lesson of the month; to secure your time slot for the month.
  • Payment methods include Paypal, Zelle, or Cash App. Cash and checks are not accepted.
  • Tuition does not include books or musical instruments.
  • Nomad lessons can be held only once. If the student wishes to conitue, they will need to chose a monthly lesson plan.

Attendance & Practice

Children, adults and seniors are welcome to apply for lessons. Daily practice is recommended. Short, daily practice is the most beneficial method.

  • Students
    • Students are required to have a working keyboard or piano at home to practice, as well as a laptop/tablet and phone.
    • If a student must reschedule or cancel a lesson, the student must notify instructor in advance.
    • If the student is young or has extra needs, parental guidance may be required, especially for certain teaching methods that involve both the child and parent.
  • Teacher
    • If for some reason the teacher cannot attend a lesson, the teacher will reschedule the lesson.
    • If a lesson is missed by the teacher it will be refunded or rescheduled at the teacher's availability.
